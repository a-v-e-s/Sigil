=============================================================================

how to make rorshach colorful:

divvy the color wheel up at (pseudo)-random. assign half to 'white' and half to 'black'
each starting point is (pseudo)-random.
shift probability determined by how likely the letter is to occur
switch probability determined by how unlikely the letter is to occur
